Accessibility rules (WCAG AA)
  • Amber & orange are fills only on light — for text, use Rust #C2410C or Ink.
  • Magenta text #E11D57 passes AA on paper/card; use for highlights & the "8".
  • On gradient/amber fills, text is Ink #241A10; on solid magenta avatars, white is fine.
  • Body text Ink #241A10, muted Cocoa #6B5A45 — both clear AA on paper.
