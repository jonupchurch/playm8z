2a NEONFORGE — contrast & new marks
follow-up on 1b
WCAG AA · contrast on dark backgrounds

Good news — the warm palette holds up well on the dark field. Every core color clears AA for normal text (≥4.5:1); most clear AAA (≥7:1). The rules to keep it clean are noted below.

Amber on Void
10.4:1AAA
Cyan on Void
10.2:1AAA
Cream text on Panel
15.0:1AAA
Hot Orange on Void
6.7:1AA
Magenta on Void
5.5:1AA
Muted meta on Panel
4.8:1AA·min
Safe as-is: amber, orange, cyan and cream all pass AA for body text on the dark surfaces. Use amber or cream for anything small.
Watch: reserve magenta for ≥16px / bold labels, and nudge muted-meta a shade lighter (#B49C6A → 6.4:1) for tiny timestamps. Never put amber/orange text on a light surface — flip to Void text there.
