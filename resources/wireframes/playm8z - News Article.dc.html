playm8z is now in open beta!

P
playm8z team
Product announcements
cover image

After months of closed testing with a few thousand players, playm8z is officially open to everyone. No invite code, no waitlist — just post the games you want to play and find your party.

We built playm8z because finding good people to play with is still weirdly hard. Discord servers are noisy, LFG channels scroll past in seconds, and randoms are a coin flip. We wanted a place where you say what you want to play and how you want to play it — and get matched with people who actually fit.

What shipped for launch

Everything you need to go from solo to squad:

  • ›Post a game — set the vibe, region, platform, time slots, party size and more.
  • ›Powerful search — filter by casual vs serious, open slots, age group, mic and keywords.
  • ›Real profiles & ratings — reputation and reliability so you know who you're teaming with.
  • ›A community forum — talk strategy, share clips, and organize groups.
💡

Not just video games — playm8z has first-class support for tabletop and TTRPGs too, including an in-person option so you can find a local table as easily as an online lobby.

What's next

We're already working on deep Discord integration — auto-created voice channels for your party and LFG pings piped straight into your server. Groups & clans are coming soon after, so your crews can have a permanent home.

"The best gaming memories come from the people, not the games. We're here to help you find them."

Huge thanks to our beta crew for the feedback that shaped all of this. This is just the start — see you in the lobby.
