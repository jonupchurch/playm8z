# playm8z — Plain-English Plan

A simple, non-technical TL;DR of what each of the 26 planned features
actually does. Numbered the same way as `docs/feature-list.md` and the
`specs/NNN-...` folders, so you can jump straight from either one to
this doc (and back). For the technical tracking (spec/plan/tasks
status, file paths, task counts), see `docs/feature-list.md` instead —
this doc is just "what is this thing, in plain terms."

**Where things stand**: every feature below has been fully designed on
paper (spec + plan + task list). None have been built yet — this is
the map for what comes next, not a report of what already exists.

1. **Auth & Onboarding** — Sign up or log in (email/password or
   Google), then a short 4-step wizard to set up your profile: your
   name, the games you play, your region/platform/age, and your vibe
   (casual/serious). Ends with a "you're all set" screen.
2. **Error Pages** — The site's "something went wrong" screens: page
   not found, server error, access denied, and a "we'll be right
   back" maintenance page. Consistent look everywhere.
3. **Home** — What you see after logging in: a search bar, a strip of
   trending games, and a live feed of open groups looking for players.
4. **Browse** — The full search page for finding a group: filter by
   game, genre, platform, region, age group, time of day, and more.
5. **Post a Game** — The form for creating your own "looking for
   group" posting — what you're playing, when, how many spots, and
   the vibe you're going for.
6. **Listing detail** — The page for one specific posting: read the
   full details, ask the host a question, and request to join.
7. **Profile + Account settings** — Your own profile: edit your bio
   and games, see the postings you've made and saved, manage your
   password/email/privacy, or deactivate your account.
8. **Blocked Users** — See everyone you've blocked, unblock them, or
   block (and optionally report) someone new.
9. **Forum index** — Browse and search the community discussion
   boards by category.
10. **Forum Thread** — Read one discussion thread and its replies,
    post your own reply, like posts, report bad ones, or subscribe
    for updates.
11. **Inbox / messaging** — Your direct messages with other players.
    Also where you accept or decline someone's request to join your
    group.
12. **Notifications + Report modal** — The bell-icon dropdown that
    shows your notifications, plus the reusable popup used everywhere
    on the site to report bad content or behavior.
13. **News feed** — Browse platform news and announcements, and
    subscribe to get them by email.
14. **Content Page** — Simple public pages like "About Us" or "Privacy
    Policy," built from editable blocks of text/images.
15. **Admin Dashboard** — The admin team's homepage: key site stats, a
    trend chart, a "needs attention" queue, and a feed of recent admin
    activity.
16. **Admin Users** — Search and view every user, ban or unban
    accounts, and remove problem content from a user.
17. **Admin Postings** — The moderation queue for flagged or reported
    "looking for group" postings: review each one, approve it, remove
    it, warn the poster, or ban them.
18. **Admin Forum** — The same kind of moderation queue as Admin
    Postings, but for forum threads and replies — also lets mods lock
    a thread.
19. **Admin Reports** — One combined queue of every report filed
    anywhere on the site (postings, forum, profiles, messages), so
    moderators can triage everything from a single screen.
20. **Admin News** — The news team's editor: write, edit, publish,
    schedule, pin, or take down news articles.
21. **Admin Content Pages** — Manage the list of public content pages
    (About Us, Privacy Policy, etc.) — publish, unpublish, create new
    ones, or delete custom ones.
22. **Public profile page** — Any player's public page at `/u/handle`:
    see their info, follow them, message them, invite them to your
    group, and see what you have in common.
23. **News article detail** — The full reading page for one news
    story: like it, save it for later, share it, and see related
    articles.
24. **Admin Settings** — The site's overall control panel: site
    name/tagline, maintenance mode, moderation rules, staff roles and
    permissions, feature on/off switches, and safety settings.
25. **Moderator audit log** — A searchable history of every
    moderation action ever taken — who did what, and when — for
    accountability.
26. **Landing page** — The marketing homepage shown to visitors who
    aren't logged in yet — explains what playm8z is and gets people to
    sign up.
